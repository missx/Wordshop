Somos un equipo de traductoras de inglés y español egresadas de la carrera de Traductorado Público dictada por la UdelaR (Montevideo, Uruguay). Nos apasionan los idiomas y nuestra misión es proporcionar servicios profesionales y de calidad que acerquen culturas y favorezcan una comunicación clara y fluida.

Translation

Trabajamos para proporcionar traducciones profesionales de textos de diversas temáticas adaptadas a diferentes mercados ya sea de inglés a español como a la inversa. Como traductoras públicas estamos capacitadas para realizar traducciones oficiales y oficiales. Utilizamos herramientas de traducción asistida para garantizar traducciones de la mejor calidad y, a su vez, nuestro trabajo siempre es revisado por un segundo traductor.

Localization

Ofrecemos servicios de localización web y de software. Adaptamos el texto traducido a los distintos mercados y culturas en que se presentará el producto. Contamos con experiencia en la industria lo cual nos proporciona mayores conocimientos al momento de localizar.

Proofreading

Un texto con errores ortográficos o de redacción puede afectar la imagen de su empresa, por eso es importante la labor de un corrector. Ofrecemos servicios de corrección tanto de estilo como ortotipográfica. Asimismo, corregimos tanto traducciones como textos en su idioma original que varían desde documentos legales hasta textos técnicos.

Subtitling

Brindamos servicios de subtitulación adaptados al mercado y cultura correspondientes. La traducción se puede realizar a partir de texto proporcionado por el cliente o previa transcripción del audio. Además brindamos servicios de spotting (pautado de los subtítulos en pantalla).

What is a certified translation?

A certified translation is a translation done by certified translator who graduated from the UdelaR and is certified before the corresponding government agencies. It must bear the signature and seal of the translator who made it.

Do I need a certified translation?

You need a certified translation if you have a document which is not in Spanish and is to be filed before a local or national government entity. This document needs to be accompanied by the aforementioned certified translation and signed by the certified translator. You might also need a certified translation for documents issued in Uruguay which are to be used abroad.

Why hire a professional translator?

Translating entails much more than speaking two languages, it requires a comprehensive knowledge of both of them, as well as of the cultures to which they belong. A translator is also familiar with general linguistic concepts, and knows how to work with different translation tools and techniques. A professional translator will deliver clear and accurate texts, true to the originals, taking every idiomatic nuance into account.

How is the price of a translation determined?

The price of a translation is set depending on whether a translation is certified or not. For certified translations, we use the fees of the Uruguayan Board of Certified Translators, which sets prices according to the amount of pages in the resulting translation and the type of document being dealt with. On the other hand, the price of non-certified translations is set according to the number of words in the source text and may vary according to the challenges the translation may present. The price of subtitles is based on the length of the video to be translated. Request a free quote.

How much time does it take to do a translation?

There are many aspects to take into account to assess the time that could take, some of them are the length of the original text and its subject. If you need an urgent translation, you can contact us to agree on a more convenient amount of time. It is worth reminding that the fee for urgent translations is different from a regular one.
